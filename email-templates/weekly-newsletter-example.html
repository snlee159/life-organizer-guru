The 3-Step Morning Reset Framework

Here's a simple framework I teach all my clients that takes just 20 minutes:

  1. Mindful Start (5 minutes): Begin with deep breathing, meditation, or journaling. This clears mental clutter and centers your focus before the day's demands.
  2. Priority Planning (10 minutes): Choose your top 3 must-do tasks for the day. Write them down. This prevents overwhelm and keeps you focused on what truly matters.
  3. Environment Prep (5 minutes): Clear your workspace, lay out what you need, and eliminate distractions before diving into work.

Try this framework for just one week and watch your productivity soar. The beauty is in its simplicityâ€”it's flexible enough to adapt to any schedule.

This simple rule prevents small tasks from accumulating into overwhelming backlogs that drain your mental energy.
